Story

The Story Behind
Straightline Creative

Straightline Creative started with a simple curiosity.

I wanted to understand what makes a story stick with people.

My journey into podcasting began in 2020. Every conversation taught me something new. The more stories I helped bring to life, the more I realized something.

Great storytelling isn't tied to one medium.

As the creative landscape evolved, I taught myself video production and editing because I wanted to tell better stories in more ways. Today, podcasts, video, and creative strategy all work together with one goal in mind.

Helping people tell stories worth watching.

I believe every great project starts with a conversation.

Not because it's part of my process.

Because understanding your story is the most important part of telling it well.

Every project is different. Every person brings a unique perspective, a unique challenge, and a story only they can tell.

My role is to listen first, ask thoughtful questions, and create something that feels authentic to who you are.

Technology continues to change the way we create. I embrace those tools because they help me work smarter and create more efficiently.

But the ideas, the curiosity, and the relationships behind every project will always be human.

Stories have the power to educate, inspire, and connect.

My job is to help you tell yours.
