Story

The Story Behind
Straightline Creative

Straightline Creative started with a simple curiosity: what makes a story stick with people?

My journey into podcasting began in 2020. Every conversation taught me something new and showed me that the strongest work starts with listening.

Great storytelling isn't tied to one medium.

That curiosity led me into video production, editing, and creative strategy. Today, those tools work together to help people turn ideas into stories worth watching.

Every project starts with a conversation. I listen first, ask thoughtful questions, and look for the part of the story that feels most honest.

Technology keeps changing how we create. I use it to work smarter, but the ideas, relationships, and point of view behind the work stay human.

Stories can educate, inspire, and connect.

My job is to help you tell yours.
